## 💳 **HOW IT WORKS:**

### **Each Card = Separate Wallet**
```
Card 1: $2,000
Card 2: $3,500
Card 3: $1,000
Total: $6,500
```

### **Operations:**
- **Deposit** → Select card → Money goes to that card
- **Withdraw** → Select card → Money comes from that card  
- **Pay Bills** → Select card → Bill paid from that card
- **Transfer** → Card to Card → Instant update
